About AiME Awards

Bigger and better than ever, the 2011 AiME Awards will recognize work from Digital Filmmaking & Video Production, Audio Production, Photography, Media Arts & Animation, Fashion & Retail Management, Advertising, Graphic Design, Web Design & Interactive Media and General Education. The deadline for submission is March 1st, 2011. The 2011 AiME Awards show will be held on April 7th at 6pm at the Coolidge Corner Theatre.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQS)

  1. Q:When do I need to submit my work by?

  2. A:The deadline for all submissions is March 1st, 2011.

  3. Q:What if copyrighted material is used within my project, can I still submit?

  4. A:Yes.

  5. Q:How will I know if I have been nominated?

  6. A:If you are nominated to win an award you will be contacted after the final decisions are made.

  7. Q:Will I still get an award if I can't make it to the awards show?

  8. A:Yes.

  9. Q:What If I worked on my project with a partner or a group of people, who will receive the award if we win?

  10. A:Only one person can win an award. But a groups name can be put on the award. More than one person can go up on stage to accept the award.

  11. Q:When/Where is the awards show being held?

  12. A: At the Coolidge Corner Theatre on April 7th, 2011, at 6:00 pm.

  13. Q:Will it cost money to attend the show?

  14. A: No, the AiME Awards are a free event.

  15. Q:How long will the show be?

  16. A:Approximately 3 hours long.

  17. Q:Can I still submit work if I am a recent graduate of The New England Institute of Art?.

  18. A:Yes, you can submit work to the AiMEs if you were enrolled at The New England Institute of Art between January 2010 and May 2011. But, the work you submit must have been completed between January 2010 and May 2011. Any work with a completion date that falls before January 1st, 2010 will not be considered for submission.
